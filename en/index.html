Welcome to the FRUE Project!

The FRUE Project is a free and opensource software project, operated by a small team of developers. All software that we make is 100% free and will always be gratis.

Here you can stay up to date with the latest news and updates from the FRUE Project. We also have a Discord server where you can chat and receive support from the FRUE team.
